## About Riverstone Family Health

Riverstone Family Health is a primary care and family medicine clinic located in Austin, Texas. We provide comprehensive care for patients of all ages, from newborns to seniors. Our practice has served the Central Austin community since 2011 and focuses on long-term relationships between patients and providers, preventive care, and same-day access for urgent needs.

We are an independent, physician-owned practice — not part of a hospital system — which lets us keep visits unhurried and personalized. Our mission is to make high-quality primary care accessible, affordable, and convenient.

Riverstone Family Health is a HIPAA-compliant practice. All patient information is kept confidential and protected in accordance with federal law.

## Appointments & Scheduling

Appointments can be booked in three ways:

1. **Online** through our patient portal at portal.riverstonefamilyhealth.com
2. **By phone** at (512) 555-0142 during business hours
3. **Through this chatbot**, which can help you request an appointment time

We offer **same-day and next-day appointments** for sick visits and urgent concerns. Routine physicals and wellness exams are typically scheduled within one to two weeks.

## Telehealth / Virtual Visits

Riverstone Family Health offers telehealth visits for many appointment types, including medication refills, follow-ups, minor illnesses, mental health check-ins, and lab result reviews. Virtual visits are conducted through a secure, HIPAA-compliant video platform accessible from your smartphone, tablet, or computer.

Telehealth is not appropriate for all conditions. Visits requiring a physical exam, lab work, or in-office procedures must be conducted in person. Our staff will help determine whether telehealth is suitable when you book.

To join a telehealth visit, you will receive a secure link by text or email before your appointment. No app download is required. Telehealth visits are available to patients located in Texas at the time of the appointment.

## New Patients

We are currently accepting new patients of all ages. To become a new patient:

1. Call us or book a new patient appointment online.
2. Complete your new patient intake forms through the patient portal before your visit (or arrive 15 minutes early to complete them in person).
3. Bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or immunization records.

Your first visit typically lasts 30 to 45 minutes so your provider can review your health history thoroughly. We can request records from your previous provider on your behalf with a signed release form.

## Prescriptions & Refills

Prescription refills can be requested through the patient portal, by calling our office, or by having your pharmacy send a refill request. Please allow up to 48 business hours for refill requests to be processed.

For controlled substances and certain medications, an office visit or telehealth appointment may be required before a refill can be authorized. Refills are generally not processed on weekends or holidays, so please plan ahead.

We can send prescriptions electronically to the pharmacy of your choice. Please provide your pharmacy name and location when requesting a prescription.

## Lab Work & Imaging

We perform routine blood draws and basic lab tests on-site, including cholesterol panels, blood sugar testing, complete blood counts, and thyroid panels. For specialized labs, samples may be sent to a partner laboratory (such as Quest Diagnostics or Labcorp).

We do not have on-site imaging (X-ray, MRI, CT). When imaging is needed, your provider will refer you to a nearby imaging center and send the order electronically.

Lab and test results are typically available within 2 to 5 business days and are posted to your patient portal. Your provider will contact you about any results that require follow-up.

## Clinic Policies

**Cancellation policy:** We ask that you cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours in advance so we can offer the slot to another patient. Cancellations can be made by phone or through the patient portal.

**No-show policy:** Patients who miss an appointment without notice may be charged a $35 no-show fee. Repeated no-shows may affect future scheduling.

**Late arrival:** If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, you may be asked to reschedule, depending on provider availability.

**Payment policy:** Copays and self-pay fees are due at the time of service. We accept cash, card, HSA/FSA, and checks.

**Privacy:** Riverstone Family Health complies with HIPAA. We never share patient information without consent except as permitted by law.

**Minors:** Patients under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian for their first visit and for most subsequent visits.
